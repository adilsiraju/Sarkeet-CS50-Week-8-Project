Who We Are

Othirakadakam

A family forged through friendship

"Othirakadakam is more than just a group of friends; it's a family forged over 4 years of shared struggles, stories, and triumphs during our Bachelor's in Engineering (B.E.).

We met as strangers in our first semester and walked side by side through all the late nights, exams, backlogs, disappointments, and small victories.

This bond — made strong by countless hours in classrooms, hostel corridors, and campus hangouts — turned into something much more.

Sarkeet was our first big journey together outside college, a chance to celebrate our friendship, appreciate the rich culture of Kashmir, and create lifelong memories in the freezing mountains we hadn't seen together before."
