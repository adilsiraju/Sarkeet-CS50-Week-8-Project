Sarkeet — A Kashmir Adventure with Othirakadakam

Sarkeet is a story of friendship, freezing nights, midnight food runs, and pure happiness. Othirakadakam — a group of close friends from college, all from the same hometown — decided it was time for their first big trip together. From freezing slopes of Sonamarg and Gulmarg to peaceful Shikara rides in Dal Lake, from bustling food streets to hidden Kashmir’s raw, majestic landscapes — this journey wasn't just a vacation; it was a bond forged in icy lakes and snow-capped mountains.
